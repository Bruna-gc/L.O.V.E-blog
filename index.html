What is L.O.V.E club

L.O.V.E. (Latino-American Organization of Volunteers in Education) was created in 2009 by Air Park Campus (Ivy Tech, IUPUC, and Purdue) to help Latinos achieve their higher educational goals.

L.O.V.E club at East HS

L.O.V.E club at East High School is a recent club that is continuously growing!

We look forward to bring new people to join our club at Columbus East High School.

L.O.V.E club Opportunities

In L.O.V.E you always have the opportunity to be part of the board and make a difference in the life of Latino Students.

Be part of something big, help with volunteering and spread kindness through education.
